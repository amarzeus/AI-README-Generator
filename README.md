Tired of a plain GitHub profile? This tool leverages the power of the **Google Gemini API** to help you craft a stunning, professional, and personalized README.md file. Simply fill in your details, customize the look and feel, and let our AI do the heavy lifting. Make your profile stand out to recruiters and fellow developers!

![Application Screenshot](https://storage.googleapis.com/aistudio-project-marketplace-data/5a471413-5d5d-4d7a-8d77-62287f32997e/screely-1721516087595.png)

## ✨ Features

-   🤖 **AI-Powered Content**: Utilizes the Google Gemini API to generate creative and professional text for your bio and project descriptions.
-   🎨 **Rich Customization**: Tailor everything from your bio and skills to your social links and projects.
-   📊 **GitHub Stats Integration**: Automatically include dynamic GitHub stats cards, streak stats, and top languages.
-   🖼️ **Image Uploads**: Easily add a polished profile picture and project images.
-   👁️ **Live Preview**: See your README come to life in a real-time, GitHub-styled preview pane.
-   🎭 **Multiple Generation Styles**: Choose between `Creative`, `Balanced`, or `Precise` tones to match your personality.
-   🌓 **Dark & Light Mode**: A sleek, themeable UI that respects your system preferences.
-   📥 **Instant Download**: Copy the markdown to your clipboard or download the `README.md` file with a single click.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Using the application is as simple as it gets:

1.  **Visit the Generator**: Open the live application in your browser.
2.  **Fill In Your Details**: Add your name, bio, skills, social media handles, and project information.
3.  **Customize Your Style**: Upload images, tweak the GitHub stats display, and select your preferred generation style.
4.  **Generate**: Click the "Generate README" button and watch the magic happen.
5.  **Use It**: Copy the generated markdown and paste it into your GitHub profile's special repository (`your-username`/`your-username`).
